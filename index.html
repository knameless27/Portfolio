About me

Back in 2016, I delved into the world of coding by creating small games and optimizing my computer using .bat scripts. My journey in the tech industry started as a frontend developer with Vue at a company in Spain. From there, I moved on to Cootranshuila, a transportation company, where I developed operational software.

Later, I joined Tu Clinica Hispana, a healthcare company with multiple clinics across the United States, followed by Noble Medical Group, also in the healthcare sector with clinics throughout the U.S.

My main passion lies in building accessible and aesthetically pleasing user interfaces, which I've honed further during my time at Cootranshuila, where I focus on creating software that strikes the perfect balance between design and engineering.

Outside of work, you can find me in the middle of the woods, indulging in manga, or exploring new worlds in games like Katana Zero. Balancing my tech career with personal interests keeps me motivated and inspired.
